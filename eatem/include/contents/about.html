No beef intended

We want to challenge the attitude towards traditional protein and create insect based food that is irresistibly delicious.

Rich in protein

Our products are based on a cricket flour with astonishing 70% protein. This also adds a nice nutritional taste. Additionally, cricket flour is also a sustainable source of protein, as crickets are much more efficient to farm compared to traditional livestock.

About Eat’em

Our existing protein sources feed us but they don’t do a lot to preserve our planet. We wanted to change the scenery because we know we can have it both ways, a sustainable protein source that doesn’t take a huge bite out of our planet. Then we discovered the super power of the cricket and our mission is to make you eat:em.
